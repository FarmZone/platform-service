About Us

A3agri is an initiative by a consortium of like-minded professionals who are passionate about bringing the positive impact in the lives of farmers. The idea is the culmination of years of experience in the agricultural sector with a vision and mission of creating a unique and sustainable solution for the entire agriculture-ecosystem at large through a simple mobile interface.

We know that there has been an upswing in the smartphone usage in the last few years and everyone is hopeful about the agriculture sector. It is the right time to introduce the effective connectivity among all the stakeholders i.e. the Farmers, dealers and the manufacturer via a mobile application.

We are an e-commerce company connecting Agriculture equipment Manufacturers directly to the end users ie farmers (who Buys from dealer) ie A “B2M-Buyer to manufacturer” technology platform.

Agriculture per say is a hyper local activity with the result the awareness and the exposure is very limited. Most of the agriculture equipment manufacturers will have a distribution channel which will allow them to interact, promote and sell the product to the end user through the network but the ability to interact directly in order to have more insight about the product performance or after-market support somehow is missing. This results into a very low repeat buyer’s ratio.

The idea is to establish direct link between the users and the manufacturer, bring visibility amongst all the key stakeholders and allow effective communication to flow between all the levels through the technology platform. With the result the unmet needs like availability of spare parts, after market support or complaint resolution can be resolved as fast as possible.

A3agri aims to transform Agri-Business for farmers in rural India. We intend to do this by keeping the farmer as the focal point of everything we do. We are building a technology platform that provides real time solutions to farmers for all their agri needs. Farmers can simply access and order for spare parts, new product or complaint resolution just over a click from the mobile app and the same shall be delivered right to their doorsteps.
